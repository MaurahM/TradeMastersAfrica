Privacy Protocol

At Trade Masters, your data is treated as high-value intellectual property. We prioritize encryption and transparency over data monetization.

1. Data Acquisition

We collect only what is essential to fuel your learning experience.

Identity Full name and professional email.
Telemetry IP address and interaction patterns.
Fiscal Encrypted payment tokens via Stripe.

2. Processing Logic

  • Maintaining the state of your course progress.
  • Executing faculty payout distributions.
  • Hardening our infrastructure against cyber threats.
  • Generating personalized AI-driven learning insights.

3. Security Architecture

All sensitive data is shielded behind 256-bit AES encryption. We conduct quarterly security audits to ensure your "Learning Ledger" remains immutable and private.

4. Your Sovereignty

You maintain full control over your digital footprint on this platform.

  • Request a full data export (JSON/CSV).
  • Instant account liquidation (Data Deletion).
  • Marketing communication opt-out.
